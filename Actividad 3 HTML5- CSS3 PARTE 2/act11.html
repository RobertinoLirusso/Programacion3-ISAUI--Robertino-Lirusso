About Austrian Alps

The majestic Austrian Alps stretch across the country, a stunning area of ​​Ice Age valleys, verdant mountains and alluvial fans in Europe's largest national park, the 1,813 km2 Hohe Tauern.
With the beautiful cities of Salzburg and Innsbruck and the beautiful province of Tyrol, which offers skiing and hiking, as well as the Gross Glockner, Austria's highest peak, and some of the best fields for summer and winter sports, the Alps Austrians are an outdoor lover's paradise.
